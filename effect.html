Effect

Extreme Weather Events

Increased frequency and intensity of hurricanes, wildfires, heatwaves, and floods.

Sea Level Rise

Melting polar ice and thermal expansion of seawater threaten coastal cities and island nations.

Biodiversity Loss

Changes in climate and habitat destruction are causing species extinction at an alarming rate.

Food and Water Scarcity

Disrupted rainfall patterns and droughts lead to crop failures and water shortages.

Human Health Impacts

Spread of diseases, heat-related illnesses, and malnutrition due to the above effects.
